Understanding Your Vitals

Heart Rate (BPM)
A normal resting heart rate for adults ranges from 60 to 100 beats per minute (BPM). A lower heart rate at rest generally implies more efficient heart function and better cardiovascular fitness.
Body Temperature
Normal human body temperature is typically around 37 °C (98.6 °F). A temperature above 37.5 °C (99.5 °F) indicates a fever, while dropping below 35 °C (95 °F) can lead to hypothermia.
Cough Status
Occasional coughing is normal and helps clear your airways. Repeated or continuous coughing over a short period may indicate an irritation, allergy, or respiratory infection.
Cold Risk
Cold risk is assessed when the skin or body surface temperature drops significantly below normal bounds (e.g., < 24 °C surface temp). Prolonged exposure to cold can be dangerous.
